Project Outline
Perfect One International Limited has invited Master Kim Ip, the founder of I-1 World Muay Thai Championship to release the Dragon Bibi NFT collection, and is the first NFT collection in the world dedicated for Muay Thai culture. Comprised of numerous cute cartoon dragons dressed in muay thai costume and I-1 champion belt, there is in total 3,500 unique NFTs in our collection!
The story
This is about the journey of Dragon Bibis became determined to change their Planet Dream. The story begins on a bright boxing ring.
Every year Dragon Bibis will collect their favouritechilli peppers on Earth back to their Planet Dream, but in 2005 they unintentionally watched the I-1 Muay Thai fights. They soon realized this is a great and fascinating sport that they couldn't miss bringing back to their Planet Dream!

Later, Junior Dragon Bibis started to realized that Muay Thai is too complicated for them to thoroughly understand given that they only watched the Muay Thai fights once only. So for the first time ever in the past 7,000 years, they called a meeting with all Dragon Bibis' attendance and formulated a plan. All the Dragon Bibis unanimously agreed to go to Earth to learn Muay Thai.

Dragon Bibis are going to evolve!

In order to learn Muay Thai, 1750 Dragon Bibis left the Planet Dream and headed for the earth. Due to the need to keep in touch with their clan on Planet Dream, Dragon Bibis established the "Dragon Bibi Community" in the metaverse.

When they went to Earth, they luckily got the help from the "Golden Robe Champion" Master Kim Ip. Dragon Bibis successfully learned Muay Thai and are evolved to "Invincible Dragon Bibis". They also successfully won the I-1 belt. Finally, before returning to their Planet Dream, they give away 3,500 Dragon Bibi NFTs for their gratitude towards Earth.

What is NFT?
Non-Fungible Token (NFT) A unique and non-interchangeable unit of data on the blockchain stored on a digital ledger. Its purpose is to establish proof of ownership of digital assets such as jpgs, videos, GIFs, etc.
